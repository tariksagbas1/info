üîß Frequently Asked Questions

The application is not working, what should I do?

Contact us by email for application issues. Share the details of the problem, the anonymous user identifier, and the device information.

The AI assitant is deeming my answers inappropriate and not answering. What should I do?

Due to safety concerns, questions containing sensitive topics such as sexual content, violence, hate speech, crime, etc. are automatically not answered. If you believe your question is appropriate, please contact us by email along with the blocked question.

I've hit my message limit, how can I increase it?

If you want to increase your message limit within a day, you can purchase a premium membership. Premium memberships with exhausted message limits must wait until the next day.

How can I delete my account?

Deleting the application will also delete your account. If you want to delete your data, please contact us by email.

I have questions about the privacy policy

You can review our privacy policy here or contact us by email.
